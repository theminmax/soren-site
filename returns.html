Returns & Refunds

Your satisfaction is our priority. We stand behind every SØREN product.

90-Day Money-Back Guarantee

We're confident you'll love the SØREN experience. That's why every purchase is backed by our 90-day money-back guarantee. If you're not completely satisfied with your order for any reason, you will receive a full refund.

In most cases, you will not need to return the product to receive your refund. We want the process to be as effortless as possible.

Eligibility

To be eligible for a refund, the following criteria must be met:

  • The refund request must be made within 90 days of delivery.
  • You must have proof of purchase (order number or confirmation email).

All products are eligible for a full refund under our 90-day guarantee, whether opened or unopened.

How to Request a Refund

Getting your refund is simple:

  1. Email our support team at support@soren.co.
  2. Include your order number and the reason for your request.
  3. Our team will respond within 24 to 48 business hours to confirm your refund.

In most cases, no return is required. If a return is necessary, we will provide a prepaid shipping label at no cost to you.

Refund Timeline

Once your refund is approved:

  • Your refund will be processed within 5 to 10 business days.
  • Refunds are credited back to your original payment method.
  • You'll receive an email confirmation once the refund has been issued.

Please note that your bank or credit card company may take an additional 2 to 5 business days to reflect the refund on your statement.

Damaged or Defective Items

Received a damaged or defective product? We sincerely apologize and will fix it immediately.

  • Contact us at support@soren.co within 48 hours of delivery.
  • Include photos of the damage and your order number.
  • We'll arrange a replacement or full refund at no cost to you.

For our complete refund policy, please visit our Refund Policy page.
