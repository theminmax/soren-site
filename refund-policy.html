Refund Policy

We want you to be completely satisfied with your purchase.

Effective Date: April 1, 2026

At SØREN, customer satisfaction is at the heart of everything we do. We stand behind our products and want every customer to feel confident in their purchase. This Refund Policy outlines the terms and process for requesting a refund.

1. 30-Day Money-Back Guarantee

We offer a 30-day money-back guarantee on all eligible purchases. If you are not satisfied with your order for any reason, you may request a refund within 30 days of the date your order was delivered.

Our goal is to make the refund process as simple and hassle-free as possible.

2. Eligible Items

To qualify for a refund, the following conditions must be met:

  • The item must be unused and unopened, in its original packaging and condition.
  • The refund request must be submitted within 30 days of delivery.
  • You must provide your order number or other proof of purchase.

Non-eligible items: Opened serum vials cannot be returned or refunded for hygiene and safety reasons. Precision applicators that have been used are also non-refundable.

3. How to Request a Refund

To initiate a refund, follow these steps:

  1. Contact us at support@sorenhair.com with the subject line "Refund Request."
  2. Include your order number, the item(s) you'd like to return, and the reason for your request.
  3. Our team will review your request and respond within 24–48 business hours with return instructions and a prepaid shipping label.
  4. Ship the item(s) back to us using the provided label.

Please do not ship returns without first contacting us. Unauthorized returns may not be processed.

4. Refund Process & Timeline

Once we receive your returned item:

  • We will inspect the item to confirm it meets our return eligibility criteria.
  • If approved, your refund will be processed within 5–10 business days.
  • Refunds are issued to the original payment method used at checkout.
  • You will receive an email confirmation once the refund has been processed.

Please note: Your bank or credit card company may take an additional 2–5 business days to post the refund to your account after we process it.

5. Partial Refunds

In certain situations, partial refunds may be granted:

  • Items that are returned in opened but unused condition (excluding serum vials) may be eligible for a partial refund at our discretion.
  • If only a portion of a multi-item order is returned, the refund will reflect the value of the returned items only.

6. Exchanges

We currently do not offer direct exchanges. If you'd like a different product or supply size, please:

  1. Request a refund for your original order (following the process above).
  2. Place a new order for the product you'd like.

Both transactions will be processed independently.

7. Damaged or Defective Products

If you receive a product that is damaged, defective, or incorrect:

  • Contact us at support@sorenhair.com within 48 hours of delivery.
  • Include photos of the damage or defect, along with your order number.
  • We will arrange a free replacement or issue a full refund — no return shipping required.

We take quality control seriously and appreciate your patience in these rare situations.

8. Late or Missing Refunds

If you've been approved for a refund but haven't received it yet:

  1. Check your bank account or credit card statement again — refunds can sometimes take a few days to appear.
  2. Contact your credit card company — it may take additional time for the refund to be officially posted.
  3. Contact your bank — there is often a processing period before a refund is reflected.

If you've done all of the above and still haven't received your refund, please contact us at support@sorenhair.com and we'll investigate further.

9. Contact

For any questions about our Refund Policy or to start a return, reach out to us:

  • Email: support@sorenhair.com
  • Contact Form: sorenhair.com/contact
  • Business Hours: Monday – Friday, 9:00 AM – 5:00 PM EST
  • Response Time: Within 24–48 business hours
