Shipping Information

Everything you need to know about getting your SØREN order delivered.

Domestic Shipping

We offer free standard shipping on all orders within the United States. No minimum purchase required. Every order ships on us.

  • Standard Shipping: Free, arrives in 5 to 7 business days
  • Expedited Shipping: $9.95, arrives in 2 to 3 business days

Expedited shipping is available as an upgrade at checkout.

Order Processing

We work quickly to get your order out the door:

  • Orders placed before 2:00 PM EST on business days ship the same day.
  • Orders placed after 2:00 PM EST ship the next business day.
  • Orders placed on weekends or holidays ship the following Monday (or next business day).

International Shipping

We currently ship to the United States only. We're working on expanding our shipping to international destinations and will announce availability soon.

Interested in international shipping? Contact us and let us know where you're located. It helps us prioritize new regions.

Shipping Carriers

All orders are shipped via USPS or UPS, depending on your location and the shipping method selected. We choose the carrier that provides the fastest, most reliable delivery to your area.

Order Tracking

Once your order has shipped, you'll receive a confirmation email with your tracking number. You can use this number to track your package in real time.

  • Tracking numbers are sent to the email address provided at checkout.
  • Please allow up to 24 hours after receiving your tracking email for the carrier's system to update with scan information.
  • If you haven't received a tracking email, check your spam or junk folder.

Need to track your order? Visit our Track Your Order page.
