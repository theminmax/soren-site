How Order Tracking Works

Once your order ships, you'll receive a shipping confirmation email with a tracking number. Here's what to expect:

  1. Order Confirmation: You'll receive an email immediately after placing your order confirming the details.
  2. Shipping Confirmation: When your order leaves our warehouse, you'll get a second email with your tracking number and a link to track your package.
  3. In Transit: Use the tracking number to follow your package's journey to your door.
  4. Delivered: Your carrier will confirm delivery — enjoy your SØREN experience!

Didn't Receive a Tracking Email?

If you haven't received your tracking information, here are a few things to check:

  • Check your spam or junk folder — sometimes shipping emails end up there.
  • Wait 24 hours — tracking information can take up to 24 hours to update after your order ships.
  • Verify your email — make sure the email you used at checkout is correct.

Still can't find it? Contact our support team with your order number and we'll get you sorted.
